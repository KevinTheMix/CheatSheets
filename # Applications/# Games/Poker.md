## Glossary

* **SPR** (Stack-to-Pot-Ratio) = stack / current pot
* **VPIP** (Voluntarily Put $ In Pot) = % of preflop (call/raise) engagement (eg 15% is tight)

## Strategy Memos

* Steal blinds near cutoff
* Play SBvsBB like a HU
