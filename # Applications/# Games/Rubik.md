# Rubik's Cube

## 3x3x3

* Step 1: Top Cross
* Step 2: Top Face
* Step 3: Edges
* Step 4: Bottom Cross (F U R Ui Ri Fi)
* Step 5: Fix Bottom Edges (R U Ri U R U U Ri)
* Step 6: Put Bottom Corners into place (U R Ui Li U Ri Ui L, with resolved corner - if any - in bottom right)
* Step 7: Finale (Ri Bi R B or Bi Ri B R, x3 and rotate top after each corner is set)
